<p><ul><li>Fused</li><li>Non-Fused</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disconnect switch market can be categorized into two types - fused and non-fused. Fused disconnect switches incorporate a fuse within the switch mechanism, providing overload protection and the ability to disconnect power in case of a fault. Non-fused disconnect switches, on the other hand, do not have built-in fuses and rely on external methods for overload protection. Both types serve the purpose of isolating electrical circuits for maintenance or repair, with fused switches offering additional safety features compared to non-fused switches.</p></p>
