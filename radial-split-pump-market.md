<p><p>Radial split pumps are found in three main market types: single-stage pumps, two-stage pumps, and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps have one impeller and are suitable for low to medium pressure applications. Two-stage pumps have two impellers for higher pressure applications. Multi-stage pumps have multiple impellers in a series to handle extremely high pressure requirements. Each market type caters to different levels of pressure requirements, and customers can choose the appropriate pump based on their specific needs.</p></p>
