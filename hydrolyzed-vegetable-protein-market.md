HVP is a natural ingredient that provides a rich flavor profile without the need for artificial flavors or additives, making it popular among manufacturers.</p><p>Another trend driving the growth of the market is the rise in vegan and vegetarian populations worldwide. HVP is a suitable ingredient for plant-based products as it provides a similar taste to animal-derived flavor enhancers.
