<p><strong>The HgCdTe Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>HgCdTe infrared detectors are commonly used in three main market types: LN2 cooled, thermoelectrically (TE) cooled, and Stirling cooled. LN2 cooled detectors are cooled using liquid nitrogen for applications requiring high sensitivity and performance. TE cooled detectors use thermoelectric coolers for cooling and are typically used in portable devices. Stirling cooled detectors utilize Stirling cycle coolers for efficient and reliable cooling in demanding applications. Each market type offers its own unique advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements and performance needs.</p></p>
