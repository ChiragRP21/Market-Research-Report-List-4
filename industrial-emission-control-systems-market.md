<p><p>Industrial Emission Control Systems market includes various types of technologies such as Electrostatic Precipitator, Catalytic Systems, Absorbers, and Scrubbers. Electrostatic Precipitators use electrically charged plates to trap particles. Catalytic Systems use catalysts to convert pollutants into less harmful substances. Absorbers utilize a liquid or solid absorbent to remove pollutants from gas streams. Scrubbers use liquid to scrub and remove pollutants. Each type of system is designed to effectively reduce emissions and improve air quality in industrial settings.</p></p>
