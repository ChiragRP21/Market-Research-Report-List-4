<p><p>Automotive fuel injection nozzles can be classified into two main types: Non-cooled type and Cooled type. Non-cooled type nozzles do not have any cooling mechanisms and rely on the surrounding fuel to dissipate heat. On the other hand, Cooled type nozzles are equipped with cooling technology such as a cooling jacket or cooling fins to prevent overheating. Both types cater to different requirements and preferences of automobile manufacturers and consumers in terms of performance, efficiency, and durability in the automotive fuel injection system.</p></p>
