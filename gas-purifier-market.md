<p><ul><li>Single Column Purifier</li><li>Double Column Purifier</li><li>Multi-Column Purifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas purifiers are commonly classified into three types: Single Column Purifier, Double Column Purifier, and Multi-Column Purifier. Single Column Purifiers are used for low flow applications, Double Column Purifiers are used for medium flow applications, and Multi-Column Purifiers are used for high flow applications. Single Column Purifiers contain one purification column, Double Column Purifiers have two purification columns for increased efficiency, and Multi-Column Purifiers have multiple purification columns for high capacity and continuous purification of gases. Each type caters to specific flow rate requirements in different industrial processes.</p></p>
