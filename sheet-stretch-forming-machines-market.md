<p><p>Sheet stretch forming machines are classified into two main types based on the direction of stretching: Longitudinal equipment stretches the sheet along its length, while Transverse equipment stretches the sheet across its width. Longitudinal equipment is typically used for forming long and narrow components, while transverse equipment is more suitable for broader and shorter components. Both types of machines offer unique capabilities for shaping metal sheets into various forms and are utilized in a variety of industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
