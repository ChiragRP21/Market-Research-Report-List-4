<p><p>The Embedded Security Product market includes two primary types: Secure Element and Embedded SIM, as well as Trusted Platform Modules. Secure Element technology provides a secure environment for storing sensitive information and performing secure transactions, often used in mobile devices and smart cards. Embedded SIM allows for remote management of subscriber identity modules, commonly used in IoT devices. Trusted Platform Modules provide hardware-based security features to ensure the integrity of a system's encryption keys and authentication processes, commonly used in computers and servers.</p></p>
