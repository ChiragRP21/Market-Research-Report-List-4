<p><p>Automotive PVD coating service market types can be categorized into two main methods: Evaporation Coating and Sputter Coating. </p><p>Evaporation coating involves heating the coating material until it evaporates and then condenses onto the substrate, creating a thin film. </p><p>Sputter coating, on the other hand, uses a plasma gas to eject atoms from the target material onto the substrate. Both methods are widely used in the automotive industry to provide durable and decorative coatings for various automotive parts.</p></p>
