<p><p>Hot food packaging market consists of two main types, flexible packaging and rigid packaging. Flexible packaging includes materials like pouches, bags, and wraps that can easily conform to the shape of the food product. This type of packaging is lightweight, cost-effective, and provides good barrier protection. On the other hand, rigid packaging includes materials like trays, containers, and clamshells that are more durable and offer better protection against damage. Both types of packaging are commonly used for packaging hot food products in the market.</p></p>
