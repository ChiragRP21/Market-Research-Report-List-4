<p><p>Professional studio headphones come in three main market types: closed back, semi-open back, and fully-open back. Closed back headphones offer noise isolation, making them ideal for recording sessions. Semi-open back headphones provide a balance between isolation and some ambient sound, enhancing the listening experience. Fully-open back headphones have no isolation but offer a more open and natural sound. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences of users in professional studio settings.</p></p>
