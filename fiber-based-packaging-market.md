<p><p>Fiber Based Packaging can be categorized into two main types: Virgin Fiber and Recycled Fiber markets. Virgin Fiber refers to packaging made from newly harvested wood pulp, commonly sourced from trees. On the other hand, Recycled Fiber involves using recovered paper and board to create packaging materials. While Virgin Fiber is known for its strength and durability, Recycled Fiber is gaining popularity due to its environmental benefits of reducing waste and conserving natural resources. Both markets play a significant role in the packaging industry.</p></p>
