<p><strong>The Injection Test Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Injection Test Equipment</li><li>Secondary Injection Test Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Primary Injection Test Equipment is used for testing high voltage circuit breakers and other high current equipment. It injects a high current directly into the primary circuit to simulate actual operating conditions. Secondary Injection Test Equipment, on the other hand, is used for testing protection relays and other low current equipment. It injects a low current into the secondary circuit to check the performance of the protection system. Both types of equipment are essential for ensuring the proper functioning and safety of electrical systems.</p></p>
