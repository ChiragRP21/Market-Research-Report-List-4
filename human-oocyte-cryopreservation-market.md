<p><strong>The Human Oocyte Cryopreservation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slow Freezing</li><li>Rapid Freezing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Human Oocyte Cryopreservation Market includes Slow Freezing and Rapid Freezing methods of preserving eggs. Slow Freezing involves gradually cooling the oocytes to sub-zero temperatures, protecting them from damage. This method is cost-effective but takes longer time for freezing. Rapid Freezing, also known as Vitrification, involves quick freezing which prevents ice crystal formation and enhances the survival rate of oocytes once thawed. Though more expensive, Rapid Freezing boasts higher success rates in terms of oocyte survival and pregnancy rates.</p></p>
