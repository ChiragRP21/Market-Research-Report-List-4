There are two main types of variable hydraulic pumps - axial pumps and radial pumps. Axial pumps have pistons that move parallel to the pump shaft, while radial pumps have pistons that move perpendicular to the pump shaft. Both types of pumps offer efficient performance and are widely used in various industries for different applications.</p></p>
