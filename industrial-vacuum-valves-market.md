<p><p>Industrial vacuum valves are categorized into three main types based on the level of vacuum they can handle: Low Vacuum Valves, High Vacuum Valves, and Ultra-high Vacuum Valves. Low vacuum valves are suitable for applications with vacuum levels up to 1x10^-3 Torr, high vacuum valves are designed for vacuum levels between 1x10^-3 Torr and 1x10^-8 Torr, and ultra-high vacuum valves are specifically engineered to handle vacuum levels below 1x10^-8 Torr. Each type of valve is tailored to meet the specific requirements of different vacuum levels in industrial applications.</p></p>
