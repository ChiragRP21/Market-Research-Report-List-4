<p><p>Laser-beam welding consumables market types include stick electrodes, solid wires, flux cored wires, SAW wires, and fluxes. Stick electrodes are versatile and easy to use, solid wires offer high productivity and minimal spatter, flux cored wires provide deep penetration and high deposition rates, SAW wires are ideal for thick materials and long welds, and fluxes help protect the weld pool from atmospheric contamination. Each type of consumable has its own unique advantages and applications in the laser-beam welding process.</p></p>
