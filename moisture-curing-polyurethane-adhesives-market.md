<p><p>Moisture curing polyurethane adhesives are available in two main types: liquid and hotmelt. Liquid polyurethane adhesives are typically used for bonding materials that require flexibility and good adhesion to a variety of substrates. Hotmelt polyurethane adhesives, on the other hand, are solid at room temperature and are melted before application. They are commonly used in high-speed production processes where quick bonding is essential. Both types of adhesives offer strong bonding capabilities and are ideal for a range of applications in various industries.</p></p>
