<p><p>Exhaust Gas Treatment Systems are crucial in reducing air pollution from industrial processes. Pyrolysis systems involve high-temperature decomposition of organic compounds in exhaust gas. Wet scrubber treating uses water or other liquids to remove pollutants through absorption or chemical reaction. Electrostatic filtration utilizes electrically charged plates to attract and capture particles from the gas stream. Rotary dust removal systems use a rotating drum to separate dust and particles from the exhaust gas. Each type of system offers unique benefits in cleaning and purifying exhaust gas emissions.</p></p>
