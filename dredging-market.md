<p><p>Dipper dredges are equipped with a dipper bucket to scoop material from the bottom of water bodies. Water injection dredges use high-pressure water jets to break up sediment. Pneumatic dredges use suction to remove material from underwater. Bed leveler dredges are used to level uneven surfaces underwater. Krabbelaar dredges are designed for precision excavation work. Sngboat dredges are specifically built for shallow water operations. Other types of dredges include cutter suction, grab, and auger dredges, each suited for different applications in the dredging industry.</p></p>
