<p><strong>The Frequency Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Frequency Filters</li><li>Active Frequency Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Frequency filters are devices that allow certain frequencies of signals to pass through while attenuating or blocking others. Passive frequency filters do not require an external power source and are made up of passive components like resistors, capacitors, and inductors. Active frequency filters, on the other hand, require a power source and utilize active components like transistors or operational amplifiers to achieve desired frequency responses. Both types of filters serve various industries such as telecommunications, audio equipment, and medical devices.</p></p>
