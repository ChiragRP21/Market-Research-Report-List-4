<p><strong>The Threaded Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Thread</li><li>External Thread</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Threaded flanges are classified into two types based on the thread direction: internal thread and external thread market. Internal thread flanges have the threads on the inner surface, allowing them to be screwed onto a pipe externally. On the other hand, external thread flanges have the threads on the outer surface, allowing them to be screwed into a pipe internally. Both types provide secure and leak-proof connections in various piping systems, catering to different installation requirements and preferences.</p></p>
