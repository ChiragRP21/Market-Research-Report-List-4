<p><ul><li>Network DLP</li><li>Storage DLP</li><li>Endpoint DLP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data Loss Prevention (DLP) solutions come in three main types: Network DLP, Storage DLP, and Endpoint DLP. Network DLP focuses on monitoring and protecting data as it moves across a network. Storage DLP is concerned with securing data at rest, whether in on-premises servers or cloud storage. Endpoint DLP focuses on safeguarding data on individual devices such as laptops, smartphones, and USB drives. Each market type offers unique features and benefits to organizations looking to prevent data breaches and protect sensitive information.</p></p>
