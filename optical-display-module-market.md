<p><p>The Optical Display Module market includes various types such as Prism Scheme, Curved Mirror (Worm Eye) Solution, Birdbath Scheme, and Optical Waveguide Solution. Prism Scheme utilizes prism technology to project images, Curved Mirror (Worm Eye) Solution uses curved mirrors for image reflection, Birdbath Scheme employs a rotating mirror for display, and Optical Waveguide Solution projects images through waveguide technology. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in the field of optical displays, catering to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
