<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electron Multipliers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM)</li><li>Continuous-dynode multipliers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electron multipliers are used in various scientific instruments to amplify and detect low levels of electrons. Channel Electron Multipliers (CEMs) are characterized by a series of microchannels that the electrons pass through, multiplying the signal with each channel. Continuous-dynode multipliers work by accelerating electrons onto a series of dynodes, each one increasing the number of electrons before reaching the anode. Both types are widely used in mass spectrometry, electron microscopy, and other analytical instruments for their high sensitivity and fast response time.</p></p>
