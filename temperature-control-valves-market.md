<p><p>Temperature Control Valves Market includes various types such as thermostatic valves, self-actuating valves, internally sensed valves, and electrically or pneumatically actuated valves. Thermostatic valves use temperature-sensitive elements to regulate flow, self-actuating valves respond to changes in pressure, internally sensed valves have sensors within the valve body for accurate temperature control, and electrically or pneumatically actuated valves are controlled by an external power source. Each type offers unique features and benefits to cater to different applications and industries.</p></p>
