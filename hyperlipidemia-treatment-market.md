<p><p>Hyperlipidemia treatment market can be categorized into two main types: Familial Hyperlipidemia and Acquired Hyperlipidemia. Familial Hyperlipidemia refers to a genetic condition where individuals have high levels of cholesterol from birth, requiring lifelong management through medication and lifestyle changes. Acquired Hyperlipidemia, on the other hand, is caused by factors such as poor diet, lack of exercise, and certain medical conditions. Treatment for acquired hyperlipidemia involves lifestyle modifications, medication, and in some cases, surgical interventions.</p></p>
