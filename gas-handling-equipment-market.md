<p><p>Gas handling equipment can be categorized into four main types - generation, storage, detection, and transportation. Generation equipment includes items like gas generators and compressors that produce gases. Storage equipment includes cylinders, tanks, and vessels used to store gases safely. Detection equipment includes gas detectors and monitors that alert users to the presence of hazardous gases. Transportation equipment involves items such as pipelines and valves that are used to transport gases from one location to another. Each type plays a crucial role in managing gases effectively and safely.</p></p>
