Synthetic Fabric Membrane separators utilize fabric materials to separate gases effectively. Track-Etch Membrane separators involve the creation of pores on a membrane surface through a track-etching process, allowing for precise separation of gases. Each type of separator offers unique advantages in enhancing the performance of automotive fuel cells.</p></p>
