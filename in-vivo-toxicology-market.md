<p><p>In vivo toxicology market includes various types of toxicity testing. Chronic toxicity testing assesses the long-term effects of a substance on living organisms. Acute toxicity testing measures the immediate effects of a substance. Each type of testing provides valuable information on the potential risks of a substance on human health and the environment, contributing to safe product development and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
