<p><ul><li>Mainstream</li><li>Sidestream</li><li>Microstream</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mainstream carbon dioxide monitoring equipment measures the concentration of CO2 directly from the patient's airway. Sidestream monitoring equipment extracts a small portion of the patient's exhaled air for analysis. Microstream monitoring equipment is a variation of sidestream monitoring that uses a smaller sample size, making it suitable for use in infants and small children. Each type of equipment has its own advantages and applications in medical settings, providing healthcare professionals with crucial information on the patient's respiratory status.</p></p>
