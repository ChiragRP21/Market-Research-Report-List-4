<p><p>The three-phase DC-AC inverter market consists of two main types: pure sine wave and modified sine wave inverters. Pure sine wave inverters produce an electrical output that closely resembles the smooth curve of AC power from the grid, making them suitable for sensitive electronic devices. On the other hand, modified sine wave inverters produce a blocky approximation of AC power, which is more affordable but may not be compatible with all electronics. Both types cater to different consumer needs and budgets in the market.</p></p>
