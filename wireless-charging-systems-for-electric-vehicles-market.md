<p><ul><li>Dynamic Wireless Charging Systems</li><li>Stationary Wireless Charging Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless charging systems for electric vehicles can be divided into two main types: Dynamic Wireless Charging Systems and Stationary Wireless Charging Systems. </p><p>Dynamic systems involve charging the vehicle while it is in motion, typically through charging coils embedded in the road surface. This technology allows for continuous charging without the need for frequent stops. </p><p>On the other hand, Stationary systems require the vehicle to be parked over a charging pad or station for charging to occur. While this method is more common and simpler to implement, it requires the vehicle to be stationary during charging.</p></p>
