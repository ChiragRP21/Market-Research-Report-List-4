<p><p>Coal shearers are used in underground mining operations to extract coal efficiently. The market for coal shearers can be classified into four main types: Double-Ended Ranging Drum Shearer, Single-Ended Ranging Drum Shearer, Single-Ended Fixed Drum Shearer, and Three-Drum Shearer. Double-Ended Ranging Drum Shearers have drums on both ends for increased cutting capacity. Single-Ended Ranging Drum Shearers have drums on one end for flexibility in operation. Single-Ended Fixed Drum Shearers have a fixed drum for consistent cutting. Three-Drum Shearers have three drums for high productivity in larger mines.</p></p>
