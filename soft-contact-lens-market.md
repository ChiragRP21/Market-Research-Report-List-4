Manufacturers are focusing on developing innovative products such as daily disposable lenses, silicone hydrogel lenses, and colored lenses to cater to the diverse needs of consumers.</p><p>Overall, the soft contact lens market is witnessing a surge in demand and is expected to continue growing in the coming years as technological advancements improve the comfort, convenience, and aesthetics of contact lenses.</p></p>
