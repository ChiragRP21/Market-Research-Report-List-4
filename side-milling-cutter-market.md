<p><strong>The Side Milling Cutter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Side Milling Cutter</li><li>Half-Side Milling Cutter</li><li>Stagger-Tooth Side Milling Cutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full Side Milling Cutter is a type of cutter that has teeth on both sides of the cutter allowing for cutting on both edges simultaneously. Half-Side Milling Cutter has teeth on only one side of the cutter, ideal for cutting on one edge only. Stagger-Tooth Side Milling Cutter has teeth that are offset from each other, allowing for a smoother cut and reducing chatter during the cutting process. Each type of cutter has its own unique advantages and is used for specific milling applications.</p></p>
