<p><p>Diabetic ulcers treatment market consists of various types including foot ulcers, mouth ulcers, skin ulcers, and corneal ulcers. Foot ulcers are wounds that develop on the feet due to poor blood circulation. Mouth ulcers are sores that occur inside the mouth. Skin ulcers are open sores on the skin that do not heal properly. Corneal ulcers are infections of the cornea in the eye. Each type of ulcer requires specific medical interventions and treatment approaches to promote healing and prevent complications.</p></p>
