<p><p>Low maintenance lead-acid batteries, also known as valve-regulated type or rechargeable sealed type batteries, are designed for minimal maintenance requirements. The valve-regulated type features a sealed design with valves to regulate gas release, making them ideal for applications where maintenance is difficult. Rechargeable sealed type batteries also have a sealed design, preventing electrolyte leakage and reducing the need for maintenance. Both types of batteries are popular in applications where low maintenance is a priority, such as backup power systems and stationary power storage.</p></p>
