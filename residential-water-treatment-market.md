<p><strong>The Residential Water Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point of Entry (POE)</li><li>Point of Use (POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Residential Water Treatment market includes two main types: Point of Entry (POE) and Point of Use (POU) systems. POE systems are installed where the main water line enters the home, treating water for the entire household. On the other hand, POU systems are installed at specific points in the home, such as under the sink or attached to a faucet, providing purified water for drinking and cooking. Both types of systems are designed to improve the quality of residential water supply.</p></p>
