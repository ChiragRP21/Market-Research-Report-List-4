<p><ul><li>Center Inlet</li><li>Dual Inlet</li><li>Offset Inlet</li><li>Center Outlet</li><li>Dual Outlet</li><li>Offset Outlet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile exhaust muffler market can be categorized into different types based on the location of the inlet and outlet pipes. Center Inlet mufflers have the inlet pipe in the center and are commonly used in smaller vehicles. Dual Inlet mufflers have two inlet pipes and are popular in high-performance vehicles. Offset Inlet mufflers have an inlet pipe positioned to one side. Similarly, Center Outlet, Dual Outlet, and Offset Outlet mufflers have their outlet pipes in the center, on both sides, or to one side respectively.</p></p>
