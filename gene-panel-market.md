<p><strong>The Gene Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amplicon-based Approach</li><li>Hybridization-based Approach</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gene panel market types include the amplicon-based approach and the hybridization-based approach. The amplicon-based approach uses polymerase chain reaction (PCR) to amplify specific target sequences, allowing for more sensitive detection of mutations. In contrast, the hybridization-based approach involves capturing target sequences using complementary nucleic acid probes. Both approaches are commonly used in genetic testing and research to analyze multiple genes simultaneously, providing valuable insights into genetic variations and disease risk.</p></p>
