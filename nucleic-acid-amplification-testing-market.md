<p><p>Nucleic Acid Amplification Testing (NAAT) market includes target amplification systems, probe amplification systems, and signal amplification systems. Target amplification involves amplifying a specific region of DNA or RNA, while probe amplification uses specific probes to detect target sequences. Signal amplification systems use enzymes or other molecules to amplify the signal produced by target amplification. Each of these systems plays a crucial role in enhancing the sensitivity and accuracy of NAAT for detecting various pathogens and genetic mutations.</p></p>
