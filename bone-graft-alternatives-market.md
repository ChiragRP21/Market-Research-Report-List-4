<p><strong>The Bone Graft Alternatives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Allograft</li><li>Xenograft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bone graft alternatives market includes allograft and xenograft types. Allograft involves using bone tissue from a donor, typically cadaveric, to aid in bone regeneration. This type of graft is considered safe, as it eliminates the need for a second surgical site. Xenograft involves using bone tissue from a different species, commonly bovine or porcine, to stimulate bone growth. This type of graft has the potential to be easily absorbed by the body and promote bone regeneration effectively.</p></p>
