<p><strong>The Prestressed Concrete Cylinder Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded Steel Cylinder/Embedded Cylinder Pipe (ECP)</li><li>Outer Steel Cylinder/Lined Cylinder Pipe (LCP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prestressed Concrete Cylinder Pipe comes in two main types: Embedded Steel Cylinder Pipe (ECP) and Outer Steel Cylinder Pipe (LCP). ECP has a steel cylinder embedded within the concrete, providing strength and structural integrity. LCP, on the other hand, has a concrete core lined with a steel cylinder on the outside. Both types offer superior durability and resistance to corrosion, making them popular choices for water transmission and sewage systems.</p></p>
