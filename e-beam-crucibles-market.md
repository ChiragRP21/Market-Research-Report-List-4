<p><p>E-Beam crucibles come in different shapes, including round and cone markets. Round crucibles are typically used for evaporating materials that need to be evenly distributed, while cone crucibles are commonly used for materials that require a high melting point. Both types of crucibles play a crucial role in the electron beam evaporation process, providing a controlled environment for precise thin film deposition. The choice between round and cone crucibles depends on the specific material being evaporated and the desired film characteristics.</p></p>
