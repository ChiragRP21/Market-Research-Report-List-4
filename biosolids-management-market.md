<p><p>Biosolids management includes various market types such as Aerobic Digestion, Anaerobic Digestion, and Nutrient Removal or Recovery. Aerobic digestion involves the breakdown of organic matter using oxygen, while anaerobic digestion uses microorganisms to break down organic materials in the absence of oxygen. Nutrient removal or recovery focuses on extracting valuable nutrients such as phosphorus and nitrogen from biosolids for reuse. These market types play a crucial role in managing biosolids effectively and sustainably to reduce environmental impact and promote resource recovery.</p></p>
