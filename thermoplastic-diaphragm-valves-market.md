<p><p>Thermoplastic diaphragm valves are a type of valve that uses a flexible diaphragm to regulate the flow of fluids. These valves are made from thermoplastic materials such as PVC, PP, and PVDF, which make them corrosion-resistant and ideal for handling a wide range of chemicals.
