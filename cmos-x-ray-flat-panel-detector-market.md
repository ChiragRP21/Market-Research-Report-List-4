Detectors with >100μm pixels offer lower resolution but higher sensitivity, making them suitable for general X-ray imaging applications. Those in the 100 ~ 51μm range strike a balance between resolution and sensitivity, making them ideal for a wide range of medical imaging needs. Detectors with pixel sizes of ≥50 μm provide high resolution for detailed imaging, making them well-suited for applications requiring precise visualization.</p></p>
