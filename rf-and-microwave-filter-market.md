<p><p>RF and microwave filters are essential components in wireless communication systems, helping to remove unwanted signals and noise. Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters are two main types in the market. SAW filters are typically used in lower frequency applications, while BAW filters are more suitable for higher frequency bands. Both types offer advantages such as high performance, small size, and low insertion loss, making them ideal for applications in mobile phones, radar systems, and satellite communications.</p></p>
