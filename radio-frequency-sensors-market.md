<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Radio Frequency Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radio frequency sensors are categorized into two types: passive and active. Passive RF sensors do not require an external power source and rely on ambient RF signals for operation. They are smaller in size and more cost-effective, making them ideal for applications with limited power availability. On the other hand, active RF sensors require an external power source to transmit and receive signals. They offer higher accuracy and sensitivity but are larger in size and more expensive. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications.</p></p>
