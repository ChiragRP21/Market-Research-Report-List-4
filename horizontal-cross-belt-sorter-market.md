<p><p>A Horizontal Cross-Belt Sorter is a conveyor system used in warehouses and distribution centers to automatically sort and route items to different destinations. The sorter consists of multiple belts that run perpendicular to the main conveyor, allowing items to be diverted off the main line and directed to the appropriate chute or container.</p><p>The Horizontal Cross-Belt Sorter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for automation in the warehousing and logistics industry, as businesses strive to streamline operations and improve efficiency.
