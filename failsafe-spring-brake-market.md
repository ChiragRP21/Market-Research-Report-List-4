<p><p>Failsafe Spring Brake systems are designed to act as a safety feature in case of brake failure. Double Spring Brakes have two sets of springs for added security and are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles. Single Spring Brakes have only one set of springs and are often seen in lighter vehicles. Both types of Failsafe Spring Brakes serve the same purpose of ensuring that the vehicle can come to a stop in the event of brake malfunction, but they are designed to meet different weight and size requirements.</p></p>
