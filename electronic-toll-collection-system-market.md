<p><ul><li>Rfid-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Dsrc-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Video Analytic-Based ETC Systems</li><li>Gnss/Gps-Based ETC Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic Toll Collection System market includes various types such as RFID-Based ETC Systems, DSRC-Based ETC Systems, Video Analytic-Based ETC Systems, and GNSS/GPS-Based ETC Systems. RFID-Based ETC Systems use radio frequency identification technology for toll collection, while DSRC-Based ETC Systems utilize dedicated short-range communication. Video Analytic-Based ETC Systems use cameras to capture vehicle information for toll payment, and GNSS/GPS-Based ETC Systems rely on global navigation satellite systems for tracking vehicles and collecting tolls electronically. These systems offer efficient and convenient toll collection options for transportation agencies and motorists.</p></p>
