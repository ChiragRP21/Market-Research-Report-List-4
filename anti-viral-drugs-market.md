<p><p>The Anti-Viral Drugs market can be classified into three main types based on their mechanism of action. Nucleotide Polymerase Inhibitors work by blocking the enzyme responsible for viral replication. Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors target the enzyme that converts viral RNA into DNA. These different types of drugs are crucial in combating viral infections by interfering with various stages of the viral life cycle.</p></p>
