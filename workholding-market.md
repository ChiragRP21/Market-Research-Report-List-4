<p><ul><li>Milling/drilling workholding</li><li>Turning workholding</li><li>Grinding workholding</li><li>EDM workholding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Workholding in milling/drilling involves securing workpieces in place during the cutting process. Turning workholding is used for holding cylindrical workpieces while they are being rotated and cut. Grinding workholding involves holding abrasive wheels in place to grind workpieces to precise dimensions. EDM workholding is used in electrical discharge machining to securely hold workpieces while they are shaped using electrical sparks. Each type of workholding market is designed to provide stability and precision during specific machining processes.</p></p>
