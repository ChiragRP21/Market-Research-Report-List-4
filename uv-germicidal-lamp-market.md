<p><p>UV germicidal lamps come in two main types: straight tube and H-shape tube. Straight tubes are cylindrical in shape and emit UV light in a straight line, making them ideal for applications where a direct beam of light is needed. On the other hand, H-shape tubes have a bend in the middle, allowing for a wider range of coverage and flexibility in placement. Both types are commonly used in various industries for disinfection purposes due to their ability to effectively kill bacteria and viruses.</p></p>
