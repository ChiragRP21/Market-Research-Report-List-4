<p><p>Plain milling cutters come in two main market types - disc shape and cylindrical shape. Disc-shaped cutters have a flat circular design, making them suitable for facing surfaces and creating grooves. Cylindrical-shaped cutters are cylindrical in shape and are ideal for creating slots and pockets. Both types are widely used in manufacturing industries for cutting and shaping materials such as metal and plastic. Each type of cutter offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the milling operation.</p></p>
