<p><p>Surgical imaging market includes Mini C-Arms and Mobile C-Arms. Mini C-Arms are compact and portable imaging systems primarily used in orthopedic and extremity surgeries. They offer high-resolution images and real-time imaging capabilities. Mobile C-Arms are larger and more versatile, used in various surgical procedures such as cardiac, vascular, and orthopedic surgeries. They provide fluoroscopic imaging for intraoperative guidance. Both types of imaging systems play a crucial role in modern surgical procedures by enhancing visualization and accuracy during surgeries.</p></p>
