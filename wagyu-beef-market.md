<p><p>Fullblood Wagyu refers to cattle that are 100% Wagyu genetics, bred from Japanese bloodlines. Purebred Wagyu refers to cattle that have at least 93.75% Wagyu genetics. Crossbred Wagyu refers to cattle that have been bred from Wagyu and another breed, such as Angus or Hereford. Each market type offers different levels of marbling, tenderness, and flavor in the beef. Fullblood Wagyu is considered the highest quality, followed by Purebred Wagyu, with Crossbred Wagyu offering a more affordable option with some Wagyu characteristics.</p></p>
