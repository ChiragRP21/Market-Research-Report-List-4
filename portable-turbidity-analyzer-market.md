<p><p>Portable Turbidity Analyzers come in two main types: Scattered Light Turbidimeters and Transmitted Light Turbidimeters. Scattered Light Turbidimeters measure the amount of light scattered by particles in a sample, providing an indication of turbidity. Transmitted Light Turbidimeters, on the other hand, measure the amount of light that passes through a sample, giving insight into the clarity of the liquid being tested. Both types of analyzers are commonly used in various industries such as environmental monitoring, water treatment, and beverage production.</p></p>
