<p><p>Invasive mechanical ventilators are divided into two main types: stationary type and portable type. Portable ventilators, on the other hand, are smaller and designed for more mobile use, allowing patients to receive ventilatory support while on the go or at home. Both types play a critical role in providing life-saving respiratory care for patients in need.</p></p>
