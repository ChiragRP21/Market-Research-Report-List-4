<p><p>Socket adapters are commonly divided into two types: 3-pin and 2-pin adapters. The 3-pin adapters are designed for use in countries that follow the British standard, with three rectangular pins in a triangular pattern. These are commonly used in countries like the UK, Malaysia, and Singapore. On the other hand, 2-pin adapters are used in countries that follow the European standard, with two round pins. These are commonly used in countries like France, Germany, and Spain. Both types serve the purpose of allowing electronic devices from different regions to be plugged into local power outlets.</p></p>
