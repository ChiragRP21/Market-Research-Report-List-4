<p><p>EVOH films are commonly used in packaging for their excellent barrier properties against oxygen, moisture, and other gases. In the blown film market, EVOH films are produced by blowing molten resin through a circular die, creating a flexible and versatile packaging material. In the cast film market, EVOH films are created by casting a thin layer of molten resin onto a chill roll, resulting in a smooth and uniform film with superior barrier properties. Both types of EVOH films are widely used in food packaging, medical packaging, and other industries where product protection is critical.</p></p>
