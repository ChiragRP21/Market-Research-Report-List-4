<p><strong>The Crude Oil Pipeline Infrastructure Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission</li><li>Gathering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crude oil pipeline infrastructure market is divided into two main types: transmission and gathering. The transmission market involves pipelines that transport large quantities of oil over long distances, often crossing borders and connecting major production areas with refineries and terminals. On the other hand, the gathering market consists of pipelines that collect oil from multiple small wells within a specific region and deliver it to larger transportation pipelines or storage facilities. Both transmission and gathering pipelines play a crucial role in the efficient and cost-effective transportation of crude oil across the supply chain.</p></p>
