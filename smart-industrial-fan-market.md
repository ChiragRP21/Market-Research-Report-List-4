<p><strong>The Smart Industrial Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fans</li><li>Axial Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal fans and axial fans are two main types of smart industrial fans in the market. Centrifugal fans are known for their ability to generate powerful airflow by turning the air at a right angle to the direction of the fan blades. They are commonly used in applications that require high pressure and efficiency. On the other hand, axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, making them ideal for applications where a large volume of air needs to be moved. Both types of fans play a crucial role in industrial ventilation and cooling systems.</p></p>
