<p><p>Hot melt case sealers are machines used for sealing cardboard boxes with hot melt adhesive. They are available in three types: Manual, Semi-automatic, and Automatic. Manual sealers require operators to manually apply adhesive and close the box. Semi-automatic sealers automate the adhesive application process but still require manual box feeding. Automatic sealers are fully automated, handling both adhesive application and box sealing. Each type caters to different production needs and volumes, offering varying levels of efficiency and convenience in the packaging process.</p></p>
