<p><p>Active Sonobuoy, Passive Sonobuoy, and Special Purpose Sonobuoy are the three main types of the Intelligent Buoy System Market. Active Sonobuoys are equipped with their own power source and actively emit sound waves to detect underwater objects. Passive Sonobuoys, on the other hand, rely on detecting sounds from other sources. Special Purpose Sonobuoys are customized for specific applications such as anti-submarine warfare or environmental monitoring. Each type serves a distinct purpose in underwater surveillance and monitoring operations.</p></p>
