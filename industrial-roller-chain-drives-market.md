<p><ul><li>Single Strand</li><li>Double Strand</li><li>Multiple Strand</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial roller chain drives are classified into three main types based on the number of strands: single strand, double strand, and multiple strand drives. Single strand drives consist of a single row of interconnected links, making them suitable for simple applications. Double strand drives have two rows of interconnected links, offering increased strength and load-bearing capacity. Multiple strand drives contain three or more rows of interconnected links, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications requiring higher tensile strength and durability.</p></p>
