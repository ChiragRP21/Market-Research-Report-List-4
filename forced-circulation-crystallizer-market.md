TVR systems use steam as the driving force for circulation, while MVR systems utilize mechanical compressors to achieve the same effect. Both systems offer higher energy efficiency and cost savings compared to traditional evaporation processes, making them popular choices in industries requiring crystallization processes.</p></p>
