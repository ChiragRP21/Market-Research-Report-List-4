<p><p>Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) containers market is segmented into three types - Stretch Blow Molding, Extrusion Blow Molding, and Thermoforming. Stretch Blow Molding method is used to produce bottles with high clarity and good barrier properties. Extrusion Blow Molding technique is commonly used to manufacture large or thick-walled containers. Thermoforming process is utilized to produce thin-walled containers like cups and trays. Each method caters to different requirements in terms of container size, shape, and properties, catering to a diverse range of industries.</p></p>
