<p><p>Hydrographic equipment market includes water level gauges and Acoustic Doppler Current Profilers (ADCP). Water level gauges are instruments used to measure the level of water in bodies such as rivers, lakes, and reservoirs. ADCPs, on the other hand, are instruments used to measure water velocity and direction. Both types of equipment are essential for hydrographic surveys and oceanographic research to understand water flow patterns and levels, making them crucial tools for a wide range of industries including marine navigation, environmental monitoring, and coastal engineering.</p></p>
