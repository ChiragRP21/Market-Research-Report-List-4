<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The External Trauma Fixator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unilateral & Bilateral Fixators</li><li>Circular Fixators</li><li>Hybrid Fixators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>External trauma fixators are devices used to stabilize fractured bones during the healing process. Unilateral fixators are used to stabilize fractures on one side of the bone, while bilateral fixators stabilize fractures on both sides. Circular fixators consist of rings that are connected by wires or pins, providing stability in multiple directions. Hybrid fixators combine elements of both unilateral and circular fixators for more complex fractures. These different types cater to the varied needs of patients with external trauma, offering customized solutions for optimal recovery.</p></p>
