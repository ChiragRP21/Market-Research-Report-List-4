<p><p>Natural Whey Protein market comes in two main types: Concentrate Form and Isolate Form. Concentrate form contains a lower percentage of protein and a higher percentage of fats and carbohydrates, making it more affordable and a good option for those looking for a budget-friendly protein supplement. Isolate form, on the other hand, undergoes further processing to remove more fats and carbohydrates, resulting in a higher percentage of protein per serving. This makes it a popular choice for athletes and fitness enthusiasts looking for a pure, high-quality protein source.</p></p>
