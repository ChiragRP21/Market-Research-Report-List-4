<p><strong>The Socket Weld Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RF</li><li>MFM</li><li>TG</li><li>RJ</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Socket Weld flanges are commonly available in different types such as RF (Raised Face), MFM (Mechanical Face Seal), TG (Tongue and Groove), and RJ (Ring Joint). RF flanges have a raised ring around the bore to create a tight seal. MFM flanges use a mechanical seal to prevent leakage. TG flanges have a tongue and groove design for easy alignment. RJ flanges use a metal ring to create a strong connection. Each type has specific advantages and is suitable for different applications in the piping industry.</p></p>
