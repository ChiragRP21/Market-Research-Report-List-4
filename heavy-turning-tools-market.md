<p><ul><li>Internal Turning</li><li>External Turning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heavy turning tools can be categorized into two main types: internal turning tools and external turning tools. Internal turning tools are used to machine the inner surfaces of cylindrical workpieces, such as bores and holes. External turning tools, on the other hand, are used to machine the outer surfaces of workpieces, such as shafts and rods. Both types of tools are essential in the metalworking industry and are commonly used in manufacturing processes to create precise and accurate components.</p></p>
