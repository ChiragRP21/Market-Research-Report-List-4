<p><p>Rotary drilling tools market includes various types catering to different drilling needs. Augers are used for drilling in loose or cohesive soils, while drilling buckets are used for drilling in harder ground conditions. Core barrels are utilized for obtaining cylindrical soil samples for analysis. Temporary casing is used to support the hole while drilling in unstable or collapsing ground conditions. Each type of tool serves a specific purpose in the rotary drilling process, making them essential components in the construction industry.</p></p>
