<p><p>High Frequency Thin Film Inductors are available in two main market types - SMD Type and Plug-in Type. SMD Type inductors are surface mount devices that are mounted directly onto the PCB, saving space and reducing assembly costs. Plug-in Type inductors are designed to be inserted into holes on the PCB, providing a more secure connection and better heat dissipation. Both types cater to different preferences in terms of ease of installation and performance in high frequency applications.</p></p>
