<p><p>Label Paper Market Types include Wet-Glue Label and Self-Adhesive Label. Wet-Glue Label involves applying glue to the label and then sticking it onto the product. This type is commonly used for glass bottles and jars. Self-Adhesive Label, on the other hand, has a pre-applied adhesive backing that sticks directly onto the product. This type is more versatile and can be used on various surfaces such as plastic, metal, and paper. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
