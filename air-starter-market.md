<p><p>Air starters are widely used in various industrial applications, with two main types being turbine starters and vane starters. Turbine starters operate by using compressed air to drive a turbine wheel, which then starts the engine. Vane starters, on the other hand, utilize vanes that rotate and create a force to start the engine. Both types of air starters are efficient and reliable, making them popular choices in industries such as oil and gas, mining, and marine.</p></p>
