<p><p>Public Cloud Service Market offers various types of cloud services to meet different business needs. Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) provides virtualized computing resources and storage over the internet. Cloud Bursting allows organizations to access extra resources from a public cloud when their own infrastructure reaches capacity. Multi Tenant Computing allows multiple users to share resources on a single server. Resource pooling aggregates resources for multiple users. Platform as a Service (PaaS) offers a platform for developing and managing applications. Software as a Service (SaaS) provides software applications over the internet.</p></p>
