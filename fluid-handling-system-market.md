<p><p>The fluid handling system market consists of various types of products including fluid meters, control valves, hoses, pressure regulators, filters, and other related equipment. Fluid meters are used to measure the flow rate of fluids, while control valves are used to regulate the flow of fluids in a system. Hoses are used to transport fluids from one point to another, while pressure regulators help maintain a constant pressure in the system. Filters are used to remove impurities from fluids, ensuring smooth operation of the system. Other markets in this industry include pumps, tanks, and fittings.</p></p>
