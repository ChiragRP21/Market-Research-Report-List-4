<p><p>A Microwave and RF Solid State Power Amplifier (SSPA) is a type of amplifier that uses solid-state technology to amplify microwave and radio frequency signals. GaN-based SSPAs offer higher power density, efficiency, and bandwidth compared to traditional technologies, making them ideal for applications requiring high-power and high-frequency amplification.
