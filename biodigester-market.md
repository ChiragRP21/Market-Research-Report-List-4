<p><strong>The Biodigester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Fermentation</li><li>Wet Fermentation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biodigester market types include Dry Fermentation and Wet Fermentation. Dry Fermentation systems process organic waste in anaerobic conditions without adding water, producing biogas and compost. Wet Fermentation systems are designed to handle liquid organic waste, such as sewage or food waste, in anaerobic conditions with added water. Both types of biodigesters help reduce greenhouse gas emissions, generate renewable energy in the form of biogas, and produce nutrient-rich fertilizer. The choice between dry and wet fermentation depends on the type of organic waste being processed.</p></p>
