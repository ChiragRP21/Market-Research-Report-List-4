<p><p>Boilers in the oil and gas market are used to generate steam or hot water for various industrial processes. Water tube boilers have water-filled tubes that are heated externally by combustion gases, while fire tube boilers have gas-filled tubes that are heated internally by combustion gases. Water tube boilers are more efficient and can handle higher pressure and temperature levels, making them suitable for large industrial applications. Fire tube boilers are simpler in design and more cost-effective, making them ideal for smaller applications.</p></p>
