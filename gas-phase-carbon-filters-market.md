<p><p>Gas phase carbon filters are commonly categorized into three types: Granular carbon filters, Bonded carbon filters, and Activated carbon filters. Granular carbon filters consist of small carbon particles that are effective in absorbing impurities and odors from the air. Bonded carbon filters have carbon granules bonded together, providing a higher surface area for trapping pollutants. Activated carbon filters are highly porous and chemically treated to enhance their adsorption capabilities. Each type of carbon filter has unique benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
