<p><p>Neurovascular medical devices market includes various types such as aneurysm coiling & embolization devices, cerebral balloon angioplasty & stenting systems, support devices, and neurothrombectomy devices. Aneurysm coiling & embolization devices are used to block blood flow to an aneurysm. Cerebral balloon angioplasty & stenting systems are used to widen blocked arteries in the brain. Support devices provide structural support during neurovascular procedures. Neurothrombectomy devices are used to remove blood clots from blood vessels in the brain.</p></p>
