<p><p>Semi-hermetic screw compressors are divided into two main types: single-screw compressors and twin-screw compressors. Single-screw compressors feature a single rotating screw that compresses the refrigerant gas, while twin-screw compressors have two interlocking screws that work together to compress the gas. Single-screw compressors are known for their simplicity and lower cost, while twin-screw compressors offer higher efficiency and capacity. Both types are commonly used in various industrial applications due to their reliability and performance.</p></p>
