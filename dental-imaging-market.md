<p><strong>The Dental Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extraoral Imaging</li><li>Intraoral Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental imaging market consists of two main types: Extraoral Imaging and Intraoral Imaging. Extraoral imaging involves capturing images from outside the mouth, such as panoramic X-rays and CBCT scans. It is useful for capturing a wide view of the teeth and surrounding structures. Intraoral imaging, on the other hand, involves capturing images from inside the mouth, such as bitewing and periapical X-rays. These images provide detailed views of individual teeth and specific areas of the mouth for diagnostic and treatment planning purposes.</p></p>
