<p><ul><li>Single-In-Line</li><li>Dual-In-Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thick film resistor networks come in various types, including Single-In-Line (SIL) and Dual-In-Line (DIL) markets. SIL networks feature resistors arranged in a single row, making them suitable for compact designs where space is limited. On the other hand, DIL networks have resistors arranged in two parallel rows, offering higher resistance values and flexibility for different applications. Both types cater to different market needs, with SIL being more compact and space-saving, while DIL provides more versatility in resistance values.</p></p>
