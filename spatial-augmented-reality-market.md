<p><p>Spatial Augmented Reality market consists of two main types: Projection and Desktop Configuration. Projection AR involves projecting digital information onto physical surfaces in the environment, creating an immersive user experience. It is commonly used in events, exhibitions, and retail environments. On the other hand, Desktop Configuration AR involves viewing digital information on a screen in a fixed location, typically a computer monitor or mobile device. This type of AR is used in industries such as design, architecture, and education. Both types offer unique capabilities for enhancing user interactions with digital content in the physical world.</p></p>
