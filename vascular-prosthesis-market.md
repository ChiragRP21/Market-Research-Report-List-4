<p><p>Vascular prosthesis market includes various types of graft materials such as polyester grafts, expanded polytetrafluoroethylene (ePTFE), polyurethane grafts, and biosynthetic grafts. Polyester grafts are commonly used due to their durability and flexibility. ePTFE grafts have a high resistance to infection and clotting. Polyurethane grafts offer excellent biocompatibility and strength. Biosynthetic grafts are made from natural or synthetic materials to promote tissue regeneration. Each type of graft has its own advantages and applications in vascular surgery.</p></p>
