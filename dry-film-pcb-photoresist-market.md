<p><strong>The Dry Film PCB Photoresist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive</li><li>Negative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry film photoresist for PCBs comes in two main types: positive and negative. Positive photoresist works by becoming soluble when exposed to UV light, making it easy to wash away the unexposed areas during development. On the other hand, negative photoresist becomes insoluble when exposed to light, allowing the unexposed areas to remain while the exposed areas are washed away. Both types offer different advantages for different applications in PCB manufacturing, making them both valuable options for producing high-quality circuit boards.</p></p>
