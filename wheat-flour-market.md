<p><p>Wheat flour market offers various types of products to meet different baking needs. Self-raising flour contains baking powder for convenience. Bread flour has higher protein content for making bread with good structure. Biscuit flour has lower protein content for lighter baked goods. Cake flour is finely milled for soft and tender cakes. Whole meal flour retains all parts of the wheat kernel for added nutrients. Resultant flour is a blend of different wheat types. Semolina is a coarser grind used for making pasta and puddings.</p></p>
