<p><p>Soaker pads are available in different types based on their composition and materials. Polyethylene (PE) soaker pads are made of a durable plastic material that is waterproof and provides excellent moisture absorption. Superabsorbent Polymer (SAP) soaker pads contain a gel-like substance that can absorb and retain large amounts of liquid. Nonwoven fabric soaker pads are made of synthetic or natural fibers that are bonded together without weaving, providing a soft and absorbent surface. Each type of soaker pad offers unique benefits for various applications.</p></p>
