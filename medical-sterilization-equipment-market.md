<p><p>Medical sterilization equipment market is categorized into three major types, including steam sterilizers, ethylene oxide sterilizers, and other sterilization methods. Steam sterilizers utilize high-pressure steam to destroy bacteria and microorganisms on medical instruments. Ethylene oxide sterilizers use a gas to penetrate and eliminate pathogens on heat-sensitive equipment. Other sterilization methods may include radiation or chemical sterilization. Each type of sterilization equipment plays a crucial role in ensuring the safety and effectiveness of medical instruments and equipment in healthcare settings.</p></p>
