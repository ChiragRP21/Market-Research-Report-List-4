<p><p>A Thermal Imaging Fever Screening System is a non-invasive technology used to detect elevated body temperatures in individuals. Thermal Imaging Fever Screening Systems are commonly used in airports, hospitals, office buildings, and other public spaces to help prevent the spread of contagious diseases.</p><p>The Thermal Imaging Fever Screening System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.10% during the forecast period. Additionally, technological advancements such as handheld thermal scanners and automated temperature screening kiosks are gaining popularity in various industries. The market is witnessing a shift towards contactless temperature screening solutions to minimize the risk of virus transmission.</p></p>
