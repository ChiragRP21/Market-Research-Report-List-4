<p><p>The sandwich panel press market can be divided into two main types: Discontinuous and Continuous. Discontinuous press is more suitable for small-scale or custom manufacturing, while Continuous press is ideal for large-scale production with higher efficiency and productivity. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences within the sandwich panel industry.</p></p>
