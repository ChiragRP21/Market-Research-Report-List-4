<p><p>There are two main types of drilling jars in the market: mechanical drilling jars and hydraulic drilling jars. Mechanical drilling jars use mechanical means to produce impact forces, such as spring loaded mechanisms or hammer-like actions, to free stuck drill strings. On the other hand, hydraulic drilling jars use hydraulic fluid to generate impact forces. Both types of drilling jars are crucial tools in the drilling industry to prevent costly equipment failures and improve drilling efficiency.</p></p>
