<p><p>Ductless Heating & Cooling Systems, also known as mini-split systems, are HVAC systems that provide both heating and cooling without the need for ductwork. <p><p>Ductless heating and cooling systems offer two main market types - split systems and window air conditioner systems. Split systems consist of an outdoor compressor/condenser unit connected to one or more indoor air-handling units, providing individualized temperature control for different areas of a building. On the other hand, window air conditioner systems are self-contained units that mount directly onto a window, providing quick and easy installation for cooling a single room. Both types offer efficient and customizable heating and cooling solutions for residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
