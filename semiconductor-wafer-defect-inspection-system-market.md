<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Defect Inspection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electron Beam Inspection System</li><li>Bright-Field Inspection System</li><li>Dark-Field Inspection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor wafer defect inspection systems are categorized into three main types - Electron Beam Inspection System, Bright-Field Inspection System, and Dark-Field Inspection System. </p><p>Electron Beam Inspection System uses a focused beam of electrons to detect defects on the wafer surface. </p><p>Bright-Field Inspection System illuminates the wafer surface from above and detects defects based on the reflected light.</p><p>Dark-Field Inspection System illuminates the wafer surface from an angle and detects defects based on scattered light. Each type offers unique advantages and capabilities for detecting defects in semiconductor wafers.</p></p>
