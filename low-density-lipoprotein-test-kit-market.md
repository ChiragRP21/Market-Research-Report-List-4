<p><ul><li>LDL-C Type</li><li>LDL-P Type</li><li>LDL-B Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low Density Lipoprotein Test Kits are categorized into three main types based on what they measure. The LDL-C type measures the amount of cholesterol carried by LDL particles in the blood, while the LDL-P type measures the number of LDL particles present. The LDL-B type measures the cholesterol content of LDL particles, providing a more comprehensive assessment of cardiovascular risk. Each type provides valuable information for assessing an individual's risk of heart disease and guiding treatment decisions.</p></p>
