<p><p>The Automotive Gasoline Fuel Injection System market consists of several types, including Single-Point or Throttle Body Injection, Port or Multipoint Fuel Injection, Sequential Fuel Injection, Direct Injection, and Others. Single-Point or Throttle Body Injection involves a single injector located in the throttle body to spray fuel into the intake manifold. Port or Multipoint Fuel Injection uses multiple injectors to deliver fuel into each cylinder's intake port. Sequential Fuel Injection precisely injects fuel into each cylinder in a specific order. Direct Injection sprays fuel directly into the combustion chamber for improved efficiency. Others include variant technologies in fuel injection systems.</p></p>
