<p><p>Marine heads market offers two main types of toilets - cassette toilets and pump-out toilets. Pump-out toilets, on the other hand, require a pump-out system to remove waste from the holding tank. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of boat owners, with cassette toilets being more convenient for smaller vessels and pump-out toilets being suitable for larger yachts and boats.</p></p>
