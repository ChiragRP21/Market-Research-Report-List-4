<p><p>Barrier films are categorized into various types based on their composition and usage. Metalized barrier films consist of a thin layer of metal deposited on a polymer substrate to provide excellent barrier properties against moisture, oxygen, and light. Transparent barrier films are made using advanced coating technologies to offer high clarity and barrier protection. Other types of barrier films include biodegradable films, UV-blocking films, and high barrier films for sensitive products like electronics and medical devices. Each type serves a specific purpose in providing protection and prolonging the shelf life of packaged goods.</p></p>
