<p><p>TMR Current Sensor Market consists of two main types: Closed Loop TMR Current Sensor and Open Loop TMR Current Sensor. Closed Loop TMR Current Sensors provide higher accuracy and reliability due to the feedback mechanism that continuously corrects the output signal. In contrast, Open Loop TMR Current Sensors do not require feedback, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Both types offer precise measurement of current flow in various applications, with Closed Loop sensors being ideal for high-precision requirements and Open Loop sensors for more basic uses.</p></p>
